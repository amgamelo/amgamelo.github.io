This is the Book.

"By her side, on the little reading-desk, was a survival from the ages of litter-one book."

This was the Book of the Machine. In it were instructions against every possible contingency.
If you were hot or cold or dyspeptic or at a loss for a word, you go to the book, and it tells
you which button to press. The Central Committee published it. In accordance with a growing
habit, it was richly bound.

Book Ritual
say aloud "O Machine!"
raise the volume to your lips
kiss the book three times
look up three times
