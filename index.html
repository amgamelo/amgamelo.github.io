The Machine is always watching.

Each infant is examined at birth, and all who promised undue strength is destroyed.
Some humanitarians protest, but he would never suit the life to which the Machine
has called him; In the dawn of the world we must be exposed on Mount Taygetus,
in its twilight we will experience euthanasia, that the Machine may progress, that
the Machine may progress, that the Machine may progress eternally.

"The Machine," they exclaimed, "feeds us and clothes us and houses us; through it we
speak to one another, through it we see one another, in it we have our being. The Machine
is the friend of ideas and the enemy of superstition: the Machine is omnipotent, eternal;
blessed is the Machine." And before long this allocution was printed on the first page of
the Book, and in subsequent editions the ritual swelled into a complicated system of praise
and prayer.

Persecution. It did not break out, for reasons that will be set forward shortly.
All who did not accept the minimum known as "undenominational Mechanism" lived in danger of
Homelessness, which meant death.
